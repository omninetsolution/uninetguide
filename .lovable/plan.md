

## Cable & Internet Independent Assistance Website

A fully Google Ads-compliant, dark-themed website modeled after ConnectivaHub's layout with original content, using placeholder business name **"NetGuidePro"** (easily changeable).

### Design & Theme
- **Dark navy/charcoal background** (#0a0e1a) with **cyan/teal accents** (#00d4ff) matching the reference
- Clean, modern typography with bold headlines
- Glassmorphism-style cards with subtle borders
- Smooth scroll animations and hover effects
- Stock images from Unsplash (connectivity, technology, home office, smart devices)

### Pages & Structure

#### Homepage (Single-page with sections)
1. **Navbar** — Logo "NetGuidePro", links: Home, How It Works, FAQ, Contact, + "Call Now" CTA button
2. **Hero Section** — "Independent Internet & Cable Service Assistance" headline with cyan accent on "Service Assistance". Subtext with third-party disclosure. Call CTA button. Micro-text: "Not an ISP • No brand affiliation • Separate service fees apply". Stats bar (10K+, 5min, 99%). Floating feature cards (WiFi Issues, Speed Optimized, 24/7 Support). Background image of smart device/phone.
3. **Problem Scenarios** — 3 alternating image+text sections: Connectivity Disruptions, Work Depends on Connection, Seamless Entertainment. Each with relevant Unsplash images.
4. **What We Do** — 3 cards: General Guidance, Connectivity Support, Independent Assistance
5. **What We Do NOT Do** — 6 cards with ❌/⚠️ icons clearly stating non-ISP, non-official, no selling, no guaranteed fixes, separate fees, informational only
6. **Trust Stats** — Resolution rate, response time, happy customers
7. **Transparency & Trust** — Data Privacy, Trained Professionals, 7-15 Day Refund cards
8. **Setup & Installation Guidance** + **Expert Support** — image+text sections
9. **Testimonials** — Horizontal scrolling cards with 5-star reviews (original content, not copied)
10. **FAQ Accordion** — All 7 mandatory compliance FAQs + additional helpful ones
11. **CTA Section** — "Need Independent Assistance?" with call button
12. **Footer** — Full disclaimer, contact info (name, phone, email), links to all required pages

#### Separate Pages
- **/faq** — Full FAQ page with all mandatory questions
- **/contact** — Contact form + displayed phone/email/business name + disclaimer
- **/privacy-policy** — HTTPS, data usage, no passwords collected
- **/terms** — Terms & conditions with third-party disclosure
- **/refund-policy** — 7-15 day refund window, eligibility conditions
- **/disclaimer** — Full independent third-party disclaimer
- **/about** — About us with Google-safe positioning

### Compliance Built-In
- Independent third-party disclosure above the fold, in footer, and on every required page
- Safe CTA language only: "Get Assistance", "Speak With Support", "Call for Guidance"
- No provider logos, no pricing plans, no bundle sections
- All provider references use ™/® with informational-only disclaimers
- FAQ answers all 7 mandatory compliance questions with original wording
